Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G Full Phone Specifications

The Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G is the latest addition to the Redmi lineup, offering a range of features that cater to both tech enthusiasts and casual users. This article provides a detailed overview of the full specifications of the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G.

Design and Build

The Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G boasts a sleek and modern design, with a focus on both aesthetics and functionality. Here are the key aspects of its design:

  • Dimensions: 162.3 x 74.6 x 8.8 mm
  • Weight: 204 grams
  • Materials: Glass front, plastic frame, plastic back
  • Colors: Available in various colors including Black, Blue, and White

Display

The display of the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G is one of its standout features, providing vibrant visuals and smooth performance. Here are the details:

  • Type: AMOLED, 120Hz, HDR10+, 450 nits (typ), 1200 nits (peak)
  • Size: 6.67 inches
  • Resolution: 1080 x 2400 pixels, 20:9 ratio (~395 ppi density)
  • Protection: Corning Gorilla Glass 5

Performance

Powered by a robust processor and ample RAM, the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G ensures smooth multitasking and gaming experiences. Here are the performance specifications:

  • Chipset: Qualcomm SM7325 Snapdragon 778G 5G (6 nm)
  • CPU: Octa-core (1x2.4 GHz Cortex-A78 & 3x2.2 GHz Cortex-A78 & 4x1.9 GHz Cortex-A55)
  • GPU: Adreno 642L
  • RAM: 6GB, 8GB, or 12GB
  • Storage: 128GB, 256GB, or 512GB (UFS 2.2)

Camera

The camera setup on the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G is versatile, offering high-quality photos and videos. Below are the camera specifications:

  • Rear Camera:
    • 108 MP, f/1.9, 26mm (wide), PDAF
    • 8 MP, f/2.2, 118Ëš (ultrawide)
    • 5 MP, f/2.4, (macro)
  • Features: LED flash, HDR, panorama
  • Video: 4K@30fps, 1080p@30/60/120/240fps
  • Front Camera: 16 MP, f/2.5, (wide)
  • Front Video: 1080p@30fps

Battery and Charging

The battery life and charging capabilities of the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G are designed to keep you going throughout the day. Here are the details:

  • Battery Type: Li-Po 5000 mAh, non-removable
  • Charging: Fast charging 67W, 50% in 30 min (advertised)
  • USB: USB Type-C 2.0, USB On-The-Go

Connectivity

Stay connected with a wide range of connectivity options on the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G:

  • Wi-Fi: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/6, dual-band, Wi-Fi Direct, hotspot
  • Bluetooth: 5.2, A2DP, LE
  • GPS: Yes, with A-GPS, GLONASS, GALILEO, BDS
  • NFC: Yes (market/region dependent)
  • Infrared port: Yes
  • Radio: FM radio

Software

The software experience on the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G is seamless and user-friendly, powered by the latest MIUI:

  • Operating System: Android 11, MIUI 12.5

Additional Features

Here are some additional features that enhance the overall experience of using the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G:

  • Sensors: Fingerprint (side-mounted), accelerometer, gyro, proximity, compass
  • Audio: 3.5mm jack, 24-bit/192kHz audio
  • Speakers: Stereo speakers

Price and Availability

The Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G is available at a competitive price point, making it an attractive option for many users. Here are the pricing details and availability:

  • Price: Starting from $299 (may vary by region and configuration)
  • Availability: Available in major markets including China, India, and select regions in Europe

Conclusion

The Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G is a feature-packed smartphone that offers excellent value for money. With its powerful performance, versatile camera setup, and long-lasting battery, it is a solid choice for anyone looking for a reliable 5G phone. Whether you are a heavy user or someone who just needs a dependable device for everyday tasks, the Redmi Note 13 Pro+ 5G is worth considering.
